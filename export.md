### SUMMARY: Section Overview (Page: General)
This paper introduces Bilevel Autoresearch, a framework that enables an outer loop to optimize the inner autoresearch loop by generating and injecting new search mechanisms as Python code at runtime. The framework is evaluated on Karpathy's GPT pretraining benchmark and achieves a 5× improvement over the standard inner loop alone. The outer loop autonomously discovers mechanisms from combinatorial optimization, multi-armed bandits, and design of experiments, forcing exploration of directions the LLM's priors systematically avoid.
